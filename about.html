About Us

This is what we do.

Research, data and evidence are not widely employed in public discussion about politics in Myanmar.

This website is aimed at promoting the use of data in public discussion about politics and provoking public debates through data.

What we’re trying to do is explaining the workings of political institutions and helping the people engage in the political process more effectively and meaningfully.

We would like to invite researchers, journalists and individuals for collaborations on making data easily understandable and working together on interesting data stories. If you have an idea you think you could realize together with us, please reach out to Htin Kyaw Aye.
